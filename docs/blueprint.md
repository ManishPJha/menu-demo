# **App Name**: Alna Assistance

## Core Features:

- Menu Upload: Restaurant managers upload their menu as a PDF or filling form .
- Menu Customization: Customize digital menus to reflect restaurant branding.
- QR Code Generation: Generate QR codes that directly link to the digital menu.
- Digital Menu Access: Customers scan QR code to access customizable digital menu.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rich, earthy green (#0046FF) to evoke freshness and natural ingredients; anchoring on the farm-to-table concept.
- Background color: Soft, desaturated beige (#F5F5DC), a lighter tint of the primary color.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#FF8040) to highlight important information.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif typeface, for an elegant and contemporary feel, conveying refinement without excessive formality.
- Clean, minimalist layout to focus on readability, easy menu navigation, and visual appeal.
- Simple, universally understandable icons to represent dietary preferences or ingredients (e.g., vegetarian, gluten-free).
- Subtle transitions and animations (e.g., fade-in effects) when navigating through the menu.
